Weight Loss Program

Improve your muscle strength, endurance and flexibility while losing weight at the same time.

Our weight loss program includes medical as well as laboratory evaluation and even includes a fitness evaluation with an individualized exercise program to improve your success at adopting a healthy lifestyle. Eligible members may receive appetite suppressant medication (phentermine) for no additional cost.

Our fitness specialist will work with you to design a program that is unique to you.
